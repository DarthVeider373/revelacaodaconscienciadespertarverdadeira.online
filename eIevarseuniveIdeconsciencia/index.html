About Uncommon Creations

Creative. Original. Uncommon.

At Uncommon Creations, we do Creative, Original, Unique, Custom, Awesome, and One-of-a-Kind. We even do Mind-Blowing, Bar-Raising, "OMG Best Gift Ever!" But we refuse to do boring, everyday, cookie-cutter gifts.

Founded and proudly operated by Yago Quintana out of Royal Palm Beach, Florida, our small local studio uses state-of-the-art laser engraving equipment and 3D printing technology, paired with uber-talented designers, to turn your ideas into unforgettable keepsakes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom order take? +
Turnaround time depends on the complexity of your design, but most custom orders are completed and shipped within 3-10 business days. Rush options may be available upon request.
Can I send my own design or photo? +
Absolutely! Message us with your idea, photo, or design file and our team will work with you to bring it to life exactly the way you imagine it.
Do you ship outside of Florida? +
Yes, we ship throughout the United States. Shipping times and costs will be provided at checkout or when your order is confirmed.
What materials do you work with? +
We work with wood, acrylic, various metals, and a range of 3D printing filaments to create durable, high-quality finished products.
Where can I order from you? +
You can order directly through our Etsy shop (UncommonCreations42), message us on Facebook, or reach out through the contact form on this website.
What is your return or exchange policy? +
Because most of our items are custom-made, we evaluate returns and exchanges on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us right away if there's an issue with your order.
